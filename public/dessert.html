Andrew Jackson lead the Indians on the Trail of Tears through his Indian Removal Act. Although this is not a sweet event at all, this was beneficial for the president. He was able to secure Georgia for himself so that he could obtain the gold discovered in that location. There are little tear drops on the chocolate cake to represent the Indians' tears and sadness for being displaced from their homes. 46,000 natives were removed from their 25,000,000 acre plots. While a person eats through the glorious, moist cake, they will be reminded of the harsh event that Jackson caused. The truffle cake includes a chocolate ganache as flavors of cocoa and cinnamon ooze in customers' mouths.
